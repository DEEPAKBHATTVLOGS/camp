Welcome to the Contractor Camp

Our contractor camp is a modern facility designed to support workers and staff involved in the Refinery Upgrading Project in Basrah, Iraq. With world-class amenities and a focus on comfort, we provide a safe and efficient environment for everyone.

Refinery Upgrading Project Overview

The Refinery Upgrading Project is a monumental initiative led by the South Refineries Company under the Iraqi Ministry of Oil, with collaboration from renowned engineering firms like the JGC Group. This project aims to modernize Iraq's refining capacity and is scheduled for completion in 2025.

  • Client: South Refineries Company (Iraqi Ministry of Oil)
  • Location: Basrah, 550 km southeast of Baghdad, Iraq
  • Scope: Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Commissioning (EPCC)
  • Capacity:
    • Fluid Catalytic Cracking Unit: 34,500 BPD
    • Vacuum Distillation Unit: 55,000 BPD
    • Diesel Desulfurization Unit: 40,000 BPD
  • Funding: Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA)
